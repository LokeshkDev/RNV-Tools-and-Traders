About RNV Tools and Traders

RNV Tools and Traders is a leading manufacturer of Press Tools, Die Sets, Clamp Sets, Jigs, Fixtures, Industrial Tooling Components, and Precision Engineering Parts for a wide range of serving a wide range of manufacturing and engineering industries. Based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, with strong presence in Ambattur, Avadi, and Thirumullaivoyal, we deliver high-quality, durable, and performance-driven tooling solutions tailored for modern industrial applications.

With decades of combined expertise in tooling, machining, and die manufacturing, our team specializes in producing reliable, accurate, and industry-standard engineering tools. From custom press-tool manufacturing to production jigs and fixtures and industrial clamp sets, every product is crafted using advanced machining technology, skilled craftsmanship, and strict quality control processes to ensure precision and long-lasting performance.

Our solutions help manufacturers improve production accuracy, reduce downtime, and achieve consistent quality in high-demand environments. We proudly serve key industries such as automotive, fabrication, metalworking, machine manufacturing, electronics, and general engineering, supplying both standard and custom tooling requirements.
