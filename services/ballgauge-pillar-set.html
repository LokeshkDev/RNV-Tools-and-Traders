Ball Gauge Pillar

A Ball Gauge Pillar is a precision-guiding component used in press tools and die sets to ensure accurate alignment and smooth linear movement between tool plates. Unlike conventional guide pillars, ball gauge pillars use ball cages to reduce friction, improve accuracy, and enhance tool life. At RNV Tools & Traders, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality ball gauge pillars designed for high-performance press tool applications.

Each ball gauge pillar undergoes strict quality inspection to ensure durability, smooth movement, and reliable performance. We offer both standard and customized ball gauge pillar solutions based on customer drawings and application requirements. With a strong focus on precision engineering and quality, RNV Tools & Traders delivers dependable ball gauge pillars that support efficient manufacturing and superior press tool performance.
